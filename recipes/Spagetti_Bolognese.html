Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a hearty Italian dish that combines rich meat sauce with tender pasta.

This classic recipe is a favorite for its robust flavors and versatility, allowing you to adjust spices to your taste.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes (canned or fresh)
  • Red wine
  • Herbs (basil, oregano)
  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef until browned.
  2. Saut√© onions and garlic.
  3. Add tomatoes and red wine to make the sauce.
  4. Simmer with herbs for flavor.
  5. Boil spaghetti until al dente.
  6. Combine pasta with sauce and serve with Parmesan.
